Disability Care
Beyond Limits.

Providing specialized medical care, therapy, and assistive devices to empower children with disabilities to live with dignity.

Ability in Disability

Empowering Lives

Disability is not inability. Many children are left behind simply because they lack access to the right tools and therapy.

Project Samarth focuses on what these children CAN do. We provide surgeries, wheelchairs, and inclusive education to help them reach their full potential.
