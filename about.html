Who We Are

A Sanctuary for the Forgotten

Little Hearts Foundation is a registered non-profit dedicated to uplifting orphans, disabled children, and vulnerable girls who have nowhere else to turn.

We provide holistic care that goes beyond survival. We are building a family where every child feels safe, valued, and empowered to dream.

Core Values

Compassion First

Love is the most powerful healer. Every action we take is rooted in empathy and kindness for the children we serve.
