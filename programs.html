Project Vasthara:
Dignity & Hygiene.

Empowering adolescent girls in underprivileged communities by providing essential innerwear and sanitary protection.

The Silent Crisis

More Than Just Cloth

For many adolescent girls in underprivileged communities, the lack of proper innerwear and sanitary protection is a silent crisis. It leads to health issues, low self-esteem, and school dropouts.

Project Vasthara restores dignity by providing high-quality innerwear and sanitary napkins, ensuring every girl can step out with confidence and focus on her education, not her insecurity.
