Who We Are

A dedicated NGO working tirelessly for orphans, disabled children, and vulnerable girls.

At Little Hearts Foundation, we believe that every child deserves a chance to dream. Our holistic approach goes beyond basic needs, focusing on emotional well-being and skill development to empower the next generation. We strive to build a compassionate community where love heals wounds and opportunities create leaders.

The Invisible Children

Thousands of children live without family, education, or medical care. Disabled children are often rejected and left without any support.

Orphaned Children

Growing up without parental love, guidance, or a safety net. They face emotional trauma and struggle to access basic necessities like food and education.

Disabled & Abandoned

Often left behind due to lack of resources, stigma, and specialized care. Without proper therapy and inclusion, they are isolated from society.

Girls Without Shelter

Vulnerable to exploitation and unsafe living conditions without protection. They are at high risk of abuse and denied their fundamental right to dignity and safety.

Why This Matters

Childhood without care leads to crime, poverty, and illness. A small intervention now prevents lifetime suffering.

Every child deserves a chance to grow up in a safe environment. By providing education, healthcare, and emotional support, we break the cycle of poverty and empower the next generation to build a better future.
